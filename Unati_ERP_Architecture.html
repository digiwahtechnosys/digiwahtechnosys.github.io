02 — Finance Module
💰

Finance & Accounts (Zoho Books)

End-to-end financial management — from fund receipt to compliance reporting

📋 Finance Workflow Zoho Books

1
Budget Planning & Fund Management Program budgets set per project/department. Donor funds, government grants & cooperative revenues tracked separately under different cost centres.
2
Purchase & Expense Approvals Purchase requests raised online → Finance reviews → Manager approves → Payment released. Multi-level approval workflow, fully digital.
3
Accounts Payable — Vendor Payments Farmer/collector payments, supplier invoices, and service payments managed. Bulk payment batches with bank integration (NEFT/RTGS).
4
Accounts Receivable — Sales & Collections Invoices auto-generated for product sales (jaggery, processed goods, ODOP/PUGMARK products). Payment reminders automated.
5
GST & Statutory Compliance Auto GST calculation, GSTR-1/3B filing support, TDS tracking. Cooperative-specific compliance handled.
✓
Financial Reporting & Audit P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow — real-time. Project-wise fund utilisation reports for donor/government reporting.

✨ Key Finance Features

  • ✓Multi-Cost Centre Accounting — Separate books for each program (Women Empowerment, Bamboo, ODOP, Nutrition, etc.)
  • ✓Farmer/Collector Payments — Bio-resource collector payments tracked with produce quantity linkage
  • ✓Grant & Donor Fund Tracking — Ring-fenced accounts for NCEL, NCOL, BBSSL and government fund utilisation
  • ✓Multi-State Operations — Consolidated books across state-level entities with inter-company transactions
  • ✓Bank Reconciliation — Automated bank feed integration; one-click reconciliation
  • ✓Mobile Expense Submission — Field staff submit expenses with photo receipts from app

03 — Inventory Module
📦

Inventory & Supply Chain (Zoho Inventory)

Bio-resource collection → Processing → Value Addition → Sales — all tracked digitally

📋 Inventory Workflow Zoho Inventory

1
Field Collection & Procurement Bio-resource collectors/farmers log harvest quantities via mobile app. Purchase orders auto-created for approved quantities (bamboo, jaggery, fruits, etc.).
2
Goods Receipt & Quality Check Warehouse staff receive goods against PO. Quality inspection recorded. Non-conforming stock flagged for rejection. GRN auto-triggers payment in Finance.
3
Warehouse & Stock Management Real-time stock levels per location/warehouse. Lot/batch tracking for agricultural produce. FIFO inventory valuation. Low-stock alerts automated.
4
Processing & Value Addition Raw stock converted to finished goods (e.g. raw bamboo → processed bamboo products; raw sugarcane → jaggery). BOM-based production tracking.
5
Sales Order & Dispatch B2B/B2G sales orders created. ODOP/PUGMARK brand products fulfilled. Dispatch with delivery tracking. Invoice auto-generated in Zoho Books.
✓
Returns & Reporting Stock returns, wastage write-offs, and inventory audit reports. Monthly stock reconciliation vs Finance records.

✨ Key Inventory Features

  • ✓Farmer/Collector Registry — Each supplier registered with contact, location, crop type, and payment details
  • ✓Lot & Batch Tracking — Traceability from farm to final product for quality & compliance
  • ✓Multi-Warehouse Support — Separate stock books per processing centre or district warehouse
  • ✓Production / BOM Module — Raw material consumption → Finished goods produced, with yield tracking
  • ✓ODOP / PUGMARK Product Catalogue — SKU management for branded products with pricing & packaging info
  • ✓Auto Reorder Points — System auto-raises PO when stock falls below defined minimum levels
  • ✓Finance Integration — Every GRN triggers AP entry; every invoice triggers AR entry — zero manual posting

🗺️ Supply Chain at Unati

Farmers & Collectors → Mobile app harvest entry
Collection Centre → GRN, weighment, quality check
Processing Unit → BOM-based production, value addition
Finished Goods Store → Stock ready for dispatch
B2B / ODOP / Govt Sales → Dispatch + auto invoice
04 — People & Payroll Module
👥

People, HR & Payroll (Zoho People + Zoho Payroll)

Full employee lifecycle management — from onboarding field workers to statutory compliance

📋 People & Payroll Workflow Zoho People + Payroll

1
Employee Onboarding Digital onboarding for staff, field workers, daily-wage workers & program beneficiaries. Documents, bank details, and role assignments captured online. Aadhaar/PAN linked.
2
Attendance & Leave Management Biometric/mobile-based attendance for office staff. Field worker attendance via geo-tagged check-in. Leave requests, approvals, and balances — all on the app.
3
Skill & Training Tracking Skills registered per employee — bamboo processing, product labelling, SHG management, etc. Training programs logged and completion tracked against program goals.
4
Performance Management KPI tracking for supervisors and program leads. Goal-setting aligned to program deliverables (e.g. # of beneficiaries served, MT of bio-resource collected).
5
Payroll Processing One-click monthly payroll run. Wages auto-calculated from attendance. Daily-wage workers paid per manday. Salary slips generated and emailed automatically.
✓
Statutory Compliance PF, ESI, PT, TDS auto-calculated & filed. Form 16, payslips, and compliance registers generated. Labour law compliance for cooperative/NGO structure.

✨ Key People & Payroll Features

  • ✓Multiple Worker Categories — Regular staff, contract workers, daily-wage field workers, program coordinators — all managed under one system
  • ✓SHG / Beneficiary Register — Women SHG members, landless farmer beneficiaries tracked with program participation history
  • ✓Geo-Tagged Field Attendance — Field supervisors mark attendance with GPS location — prevents buddy punching
  • ✓Manday Calculation — System auto-calculates total mandays generated (a key KPI for government reporting)
  • ✓DBT / Direct Benefit Transfer — Daily-wage payments directly to worker bank accounts via integrated payment gateway
  • ✓Payroll → Finance Integration — Approved payroll auto-creates journal entries in Zoho Books — zero double entry

06 — Cross-Module Integration
🔗

How the Modules Talk to Each Other

No silos — every action in one module automatically updates the others

📦 + 💰 Inventory ↔ Finance

  • →Goods Receipt Note (GRN) auto-creates Vendor Bill in Finance
  • →Sales Order Dispatch auto-raises Sales Invoice in Books
  • →Approved PO creates Budget Commitment — no over-spending
  • →Stock Valuation feeds Balance Sheet (inventory asset value)

👥 + 💰 Payroll ↔ Finance

  • →Approved Payroll Run auto-posts Salary Journal Entry in Books
  • →Bank payment instruction sent directly to bank via integration
  • →PF/ESI/TDS deductions auto-create statutory liability entries
  • →Department-wise salary cost mapped to cost centres for program P&L

👥 + 📦 People ↔ Inventory

  • →Field worker attendance linked to collection entries — productivity tracking
  • →Collector performance (MT collected) available in HR performance module
  • →Daily-wage payroll calculated from manday attendance in inventory shifts
  • →Skill certifications determine task assignments in processing unit
Example End-to-End Workflow: Bio-Resource Collection Cycle A field supervisor logs 500 kg bamboo collected via mobile app (Inventory module) → System raises Purchase Order for farmer payment → Finance creates Vendor Bill → HR marks field worker attendance & mandays → Payroll calculates daily wage → Bank payment released → Management sees collection volume vs. cost on the unified dashboard. All automatically. Zero paper.
