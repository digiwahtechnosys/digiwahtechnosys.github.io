One delivery, from sales order to invoice — eight stages.

Driver logs into the private portal — sees only jobs assigned to them — taps "Navigate" to open Google Maps.

Dispatcher tracks drivers live on the map

While jobs are active, driver phones send GPS coordinates periodically. Dispatch screen shows live driver positions alongside delivery pins — so the office always knows who is where.

Driver completes delivery

At site, driver uploads POD photo, captures customer signature on-screen, adds notes, taps Complete.

Qty delivered = qty ordered → Delivered. Partial qty → Part Delivered, remaining qty stays outstanding.

Photo + signature + timestamp + driver name pushed back to Unleashed. Only fully-delivered jobs flag as "Ready to Invoice".

What's already built (Sprints 1 & 2)

Available for you to test right now in the Climat app.

  • Driver records — name, phone (+61), rego, vehicle type, active status. 3 drivers seeded.
  • Delivery Job records — 14 fields including line items, qty ordered / to deliver / delivered, status workflow.
  • Driver portal — private, invite-only. Each driver sees only their own deliveries.
  • Google Maps Navigate button — one tap on the job opens turn-by-turn directions in the phone's Maps app.
  • Job Completion form — POD photo upload, on-screen customer signature, delivery notes.
  • Auto-update on completion — status set to Delivered, delivered time recorded, notes copied back to the job.

Sprint 3 — dispatch board & partial delivery logic

Two things: the drag-and-drop scheduling board, and the partial-qty guardrails on invoicing.

  • Drag-and-drop dispatch board — custom page. Unassigned jobs on the left. Driver columns on the right. Drag a card onto a driver → driver is assigned and status moves to Assigned.
  • Partial delivery qty logic — dispatcher sets "Qty to Deliver Today" per line. Driver records actual qty delivered. Status auto-sets to Part Delivered or Delivered based on the difference.
  • Invoice safety rule — a job is only flagged "Ready to Invoice" when every line is fully delivered. Partial jobs stay outstanding until completed.
  • "Mark Complete" button on driver app — pre-fills the Job Completion form so drivers don't re-type the job reference.

What comes after Sprint 3

Listed so you can see the full picture of the build.

  • Sprint 4 — Unleashed integration — auto-create jobs from sales orders, push POD back. Requires Unleashed API access credentials from your side.
  • Sprint 5 — Live driver map — driver phones send GPS at intervals while on a job; dispatch sees live positions and delivery pins. Requires: (1) a Google Maps API key set up on your Google account, and (2) drivers granting location permission on their phones.
